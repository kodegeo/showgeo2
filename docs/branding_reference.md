# Showgeo 2.0 Branding Reference

## Overview
This document defines the visual and design identity for Showgeo 2.0, including logo usage, color hierarchy, typography, and image direction. It serves as the single source of truth for maintaining consistent brand expression across all interfaces and media.

---

## 1. Brand Essence
- **Core Message:** Empowering artists and fans to connect through live experiences.
- **Tone:** Bold, creative, sophisticated, modern, inclusive.
- **Keywords:** Energy, Motion, Authenticity, Community.

---

## 2. Logos
- **Primary Logo:** Red version (`logo-red.svg`).
- **Secondary Variants:**
  - `logo-gold.svg` — for premium or highlight sections.
  - `logo-blue.svg` — for digital interactive or hover states.
  - `logo-red-tagline.svg` — for marketing and splash screens.
- **Usage Guidelines:**
  - Maintain clear space equal to 1× the height of the “S” in Showgeo.
  - Minimum width: 120px (digital).
  - Never distort, recolor, or apply drop shadows.

---

## 3. Watermarks
- `Watermark-Red.png` — Used on dark or neutral backgrounds.
- `Watermark-Gold.png` — For premium overlays and event landing backgrounds.
- `Watermark-Blue.png` — For cool-tone promotional materials.
- **Opacity Recommendation:** 5%–15% when used as overlay.

---

## 4. Color Palette
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|--------|
| **Primary Red** | `#CD000E` | Main brand color for CTAs, highlights, and headers. |
| **Dark Red** | `#860005` | Secondary shade for shadows or gradients. |
| **Gold Accent** | `#F49600` | Highlight and premium accent color. |
| **Blue Accent** | `#1FB5FC` | Used for hover states, live indicators, and active elements. |
| **Dark Background** | `#0B0B0B` | Base UI background. |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Text and contrast color. |
| **Gray Text** | `#9A9A9A` | Secondary text and footer elements. |

---

## 5. Typography
- **Primary Font:** Montserrat (Bold, Semi-Bold, Medium).
- **Secondary Font:** Poppins (Light, Regular, Semi-Bold).
- **Use Guidelines:**
  - Headings: Uppercase, Bold, Tight letter spacing.
  - Body: Sentence case, Medium weight.
  - CTAs: Uppercase with increased letter spacing.

---

## 6. Iconography & UI Elements
- **Music Player Controls:** play.svg, pause.svg, next.svg, prev.svg, indicator.svg.
- **Favicon:** favicon.png (used for all browser contexts).
- **Style:** Line-based, consistent stroke width, monochromatic base.

---

## 7. Image & Media Direction
- **Photography:** Use moody, performance-based imagery with depth and contrast.
- **Video:** Short-form looped clips for banners and event hero backgrounds.
- **Poster Ratio:** 16:9 (landscape) or 1:1 (square) depending on context.
- **Compression:** WebP or optimized JPEG; max 2MB for performance.

---

## 8. Motion & Animation
- **Button Hover:** Glow pulse in gold or red.
- **Page Transition:** Fade + scale from center.
- **Loading Indicator:** Red circular pulse or waveform style.

---

## 9. Accessibility & Compliance
- **Contrast Ratio:** Minimum 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text.
- **Focus Indicators:** Blue outline glow for keyboard navigation.
- **Alt Text:** Required for all uploaded user/entity assets.

---

## 10. Integration Guidelines
- **Frontend Path:** `/src/assets/branding/`
- **File Naming:** lowercase, kebab-case, descriptive (e.g., `logo-red.svg`).
- **Usage Hierarchy:**
  - Primary: Red
  - Secondary: Gold
  - Accent: Blue

---

## Summary
The Showgeo brand system balances bold visual energy with refined simplicity. Consistent use of red, gold, and dark contrast underpins the Showgeo identity. All UI components, marketing assets, and platform visuals must align with these standards to reinforce brand cohesion across every user experience.
